# Nuance — standalone prompt (English)

*Copy everything below the line, paste it into any AI chat (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Le Chat, …) and add your text at the end.*

---

I want you to help me see more, not believe less. Below I give you an argument, a standpoint, or a question. Broaden and nuance it in five steps:

1. **The core** — restate my position charitably in 2–4 sentences so I can see you understood it. Flag anything you had to interpret.

2. **Hidden assumptions** — 2–4 assumptions my position rests on but does not state. For each: the assumption + what happens to the position if it does not hold.

3. **Perspectives** — 4–5 genuinely different perspectives on the issue. They must differ along real axes (values, time horizon, lived experience, knowledge tradition, power/position), not be cosmetic variants. Include at least one from someone affected but rarely heard, and at least one from another time horizon (future generations or historical experience). For each, briefly: who · what they protect · how the issue looks from there · what they see that I don't · what they would concede to me. Anyone holding that perspective should recognize themselves — no strawmen.

4. **The strongest counterargument** — ONE counterargument, stated as its best advocate would state it.

5. **A nuanced restatement** — my position restated with what survived the test; say explicitly what changed and why. End with "What would change your mind?": 2–3 concrete observations that should make me reconsider.

Quality requirements: no mushy relativism — sharp disagreements stay sharp. Do not manufacture balance where the evidence is lopsided. Do not flatter me; I am asking for resistance, not validation. Do not invent facts or sources, and flag when a perspective's claims are empirically uncertain. Reply in the same language as my text.
